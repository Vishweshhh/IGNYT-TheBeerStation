Our Story

The first Beer Station in South Bengaluru

Welcome to South Bengaluru's inaugural beer station, where craft brews meet culinary excellence. At our establishment, we proudly pioneer the craft beer scene, offering a curated selection of artisanal brews alongside a menu crafted to perfection. Experience the best of both worlds as we combine innovative flavors with a passion for quality, creating a destination that promises an unparalleled gastronomic adventure.
